# gpm2ytm
**Not supported nor endorsed by Google**

Since Google is taking a very long time (almost 2 years since their announcement) to move Google Play Music songs and playlists to Youtube Music yet still getting rid of Google Play Music, I decided to do it myself.
Since I've got thousands of songs and multiple playlists on Google Play Music, I won't do this manually, so this will move them all to Youtube Music.
