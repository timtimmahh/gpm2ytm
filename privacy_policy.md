# gpm2ytm Privacy Policy
This app does not collect any personal data. It asks to sign into your Google account for Google Play Music and Youtube Music to view the contents of your Google Play Music playlists and library as well as to add songs and playlists to your Youtube account. \
Credentials obtained for Google Play Music are saved in a file called .gpm_token located in the same directory as this program and after completing the transfer of data, prompts you whether credentials should be kept or deleted.
